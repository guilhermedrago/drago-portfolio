Guilherme Drago

Wolfgang Weingart

Was a German graphic designer and typographer who was known as "the father" of New Wave or Swiss Punk typography. This work consisted on creating a fanzine inspired on his works.

Style

Strong, contrasting colors to catch the audience's attention There are extra elements to “decorate” the poster. Text misalignments (new wave) but at the same time very influenced by the Swiss style. Simple sans-serif typography, but at the same time worked particularly in the title. Ornamentation (give value to unimportant elements).
