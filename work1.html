About Us

Doces Coloridos is a small company founded by a couple who, after facing difficulties in finding an affordable party organizer for their daughter’s birthday, were inspired to start their own business. Their mission is to provide affordable children's party services, including sweets, cakes, decorations, and face-painting, with a special emphasis on creating visually stunning cakes. I had the privilege of designing the logo for Doces Coloridos, ensuring it reflected the brand’s joyful and vibrant essence, in harmony with their goal of creating memorable and delightful experiences.
